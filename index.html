About Us

At Aussie Built Homes, we believe that your home is more than just a place—it's where your story begins. With a passion for craftsmanship and an unwavering commitment to quality, we work alongside you to turn your vision into reality.

FAQ

Got Questions? We've Got the Answers You Need

Got Questions? We've Got the Answers You Need

Do you offer custom home designs?

Yes, we collaborate with you to design a home tailored to your lifestyle, preferences, and budget—from the initial concept through to completion.

How long does the home building process take?

Timelines vary based on design complexity and site conditions, but most builds take between 6 to 12 months from approval to handover.

What’s included in the base price of a home?

Our base price includes a comprehensive range of quality inclusions—fixtures, fittings, and finishes. We provide a detailed list upfront for full transparency.

What kind of warranty do you offer?

We provide a 6-year structural warranty covering major defects that impact the integrity of your home. This includes critical components such as foundations, load-bearing walls, roofing, waterproofing, and fire safety systems—ensuring long-term peace of mind.

Do you help with council approvals?

Absolutely. We handle all necessary applications and approvals to ensure a smooth building process from start to finish.

Can I bring my own plans?

Yes, we’re happy to work with existing plans or help refine them to suit your land and budget.

How do I get started?

Contact us via phone 1300 361 325, email [email protected], or our enquiry form. We’ll book a free consultation to understand your goals and outline the next steps.
